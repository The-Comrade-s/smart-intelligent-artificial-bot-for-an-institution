# COSIB User Manual

For students and lecturers of the Computer Science Department, Gateway ICT
Polytechnic, Saapade.

## Getting started

1. Go to the COSIB landing page and click **Start Chatting**.
2. Register with your name, a username, your email, and a password (matric
   number is optional). Lecturer accounts are created or upgraded by an
   administrator — register as a student first, then ask the department to
   upgrade your role.
3. You're taken straight to the chat.

## Chatting with COSIB

- Type a question in plain language — no special syntax needed.
- The quick-action cards on the empty chat screen are shortcuts for common
  requests (explaining a concept, course info, lecturer info, announcements,
  programming help, quiz generation, study tips).
- COSIB automatically figures out whether your question is about the
  **department** (courses, lecturers, registration, policies), an
  **academic** concept (programming, algorithms, databases, etc), or
  **campus life** in general — you don't need to specify which.
- If COSIB isn't sure about something institution-specific, it says so
  rather than guessing, and suggests confirming with the department.

### During a response

- **Copy** — copies the full response to your clipboard.
- **Thumbs up / down** — tells the department how COSIB is doing; ratings
  feed into the admin feedback dashboard.
- Follow-up suggestions appear under a response — click one to continue
  the conversation.

### Managing conversations

- **New Chat** starts a fresh conversation.
- The sidebar lists your past conversations — search by title, click to
  reopen, hover to pin (keeps it at the top) or delete.
- Conversations are private to your account.

### Uploading documents

Ask COSIB to help with lecture notes: upload a PDF, DOCX, or TXT file (up
to 15MB) and ask it to summarize, explain, generate a quiz, generate
flashcards, or answer a specific question — grounded only in what's in
that document.

## For lecturers

Everything above, plus:
- **Publish announcements** — visible to students once published.
- **Create and manage events** — seminars, exam schedules, SIWES orientation, etc.

## Submitting feedback

Use the thumbs up/down on any response, or ask COSIB to help you reach the
feedback form if you want to leave a longer comment or report a bug —
administrators see every submission and can respond directly.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| Can't log in | Confirm your email/password; contact an administrator if your account was suspended |
| COSIB gives an unexpected/placeholder-sounding answer | The department may not have activated a live AI provider yet — flag it via feedback |
| A response seems wrong | Use thumbs down and add a comment via feedback — this is exactly what that channel is for |
